GymIcon is Chinas first social media platform dedicated to fitness and health. GymIcon is a community driven platform of key opinion leaders in field of fitness and wellness and open to all fitness aficionados to join. Members can open their own profile and share their fitness secrets as well as products they use within the community.

Creators Welcome

Featuring fully social media features interaction with the KOL’s is easy and fun, from following your tribe to posting messages and direct chat interaction, GymIcon is a state of the art social media app. Newest html5 technology is able to integrate the application fully in existing social media networks, like facebook, instagram, weixin and weibo. Apart from sharing multimedia content, from photos to videos, from text to voice messages, users of GymIcon can also easily buy products and services shared by the KOLs within the app creating a unique social driven e-commerce environment. GymIcon will be available to download from the app store in early 2015 with and Android version to follow soon.
